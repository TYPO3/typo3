..  _add-custom-logo:

Add a custom logo
=================

The Camino logo is at the top of page and in the footer.

To change the logo, select the root page of your site:
:guilabel:`Root Page > Page Properties > Appearance > Logo`

..  figure:: /Images/CustomizeLogo.png
    :class: with-border
    :alt: Screenshot of the logo appearance tab
    :zoom: lightbox

Save your changes. Then `clear the cache <https://docs.typo3.org/permalink/t3start:how-to-clear-cache>`_.
