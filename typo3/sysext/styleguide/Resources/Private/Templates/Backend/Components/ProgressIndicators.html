Progress bars represent a specific state that can be displayed in a percental fashion, e.g. amount of uploaded files, executed Upgrade Wizards or disk space usage.
If the exact amount of data being loaded is not known, progress bars may be rendered in an indeterminate state.

If applicable, use clear wording that describes the progress bar.
